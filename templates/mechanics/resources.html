Overview

Resources are produced by provinces and used for projects, military, and trade. Each resource has specific uses and may be more or less valuable depending on the market.

FAQ: My resource totals didn't change after buying/selling

If your resource totals don't immediately reflect purchases or sales, try the following:

  • Refresh the page — some UI data is cached in the browser and updates on reload.
  • Check the Market page and your Country page to confirm the transaction succeeded (offers show in your trade history).
  • Some changes are applied inside background tasks (e.g., supply/regeneration). Wait a short moment and re-check or trigger a manual action (buy/sell) to force an update.
  • If the problem persists, note the exact steps (what you bought/sold, province, and quantities) and report it — we'll investigate and fix any server-side application bug.

Key resources

  • Rations - food: sustains population and growth.
  • Oil / Gasoline - fuels military and projects.
  • Steel / Iron / Components - used for infrastructure, upgrades, and military units.
  • Consumer Goods - increases tax income (see Consumer Goods page).
