rocket Ballistic Missiles (ICBMs)

Long-range missiles that devastate enemy infrastructure and military units.

Cost$16M + 550 Steel
Build LimitSilos + 1 (can build 1 without silos)
Launch RequirementNeed at least 1 Silo to launch
TargetEnemy military units + random province damage

Note: You can purchase 1 ICBM from the Military page without any silos, but you need silos to store more and to actually launch them in war.

dangerous Nuclear Weapons (Nukes)

The ultimate weapon - causes massive destruction to infrastructure and morale.

Cost$80M + 1,200 Uranium + 900 Steel
Build Limit1 per Silo (requires silos)
Launch RequirementNeed at least 1 Silo to launch
TargetEnemy military + severe province destruction

Note: You cannot build nukes without silos. Each silo allows you to store 1 nuke.

construction Building Silos

Silos are military infrastructure built in your provinces.

Build Cost$21M + 540 Steel + 240 Aluminium
LocationProvince → Infrastructure → Military tab
Provides+1 ICBM capacity, +1 Nuke capacity

tips_and_updates WMD Projects

National projects enhance your WMD capabilities.

Ballistic Missile Silo$280M - Required before building any ICBMs
ICBM Silo$355M - Increases ICBM effectiveness
Nuclear Testing Facility$575M - Required before building nukes

warning How to Use WMDs in War

  1. Declare war on an enemy nation
  2. Go to the Wars page and select the active war
  3. Click "Attack" to enter combat
  4. Select "Special Attack" (missiles/nukes option)
  5. Choose your WMD and target enemy units
  6. WMDs consume 1 unit per launch and damage random enemy province
