Declaring War

You can declare war on countries within allowed province difference and when not under truce. Declare from the Wars page; the game enforces truce timers and province bounds.

Combat basics

Wars use morale and supplies. Attacks cost supplies; morale loss may lead to surrender and resource transfers. Upgrades and unit composition change the effectiveness of armies.

Best practices

  • Ensure sufficient supplies before large offensives.
  • Scout enemy strengths and aim for favorable matchups.
  • Use coalitions to coordinate attacks and defense.
