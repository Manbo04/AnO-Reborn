What are Consumer Goods?

Consumer goods are advanced manufactured items that your population consumes. They increase tax income and stability: the more consumer goods your population consumes (up to supply), the higher your per-capita tax yield.

How to produce them

Build and upgrade infrastructure that produces or refines materials into consumer goods (factories, component lines). Projects and upgrades can increase consumer goods production efficiency.

Practical tips

  • Match consumer goods to population: roughly 1 consumer good per ~80,000 people improves tax yield.
  • Sell surplus consumer goods on the market for gold when prices are favourable.
