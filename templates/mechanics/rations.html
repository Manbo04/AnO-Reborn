info Rations Overview

description What Are Rations?

Rations are the most critical resource in the game. They represent food that your population consumes to survive and grow. Without sufficient rations, your population cannot grow and your tax income suffers.

PurposeFeed your population
If SufficientPopulation grows
If InsufficientNo growth, up to -30% taxes

schedule When Do Rations Update?

Rations are processed hourly by two separate tasks:

ProductionEvery hour at :25
ConsumptionEvery hour at :45

Your rations will increase at :25 (farms produce), then decrease at :45 (population eats).

trending_down Rations Consumption

calculate Consumption Formula

Each province consumes rations based on its population:

Rate1 ration per 50,000 population
Minimum1 ration per province
TimingEvery hour at :45

Important: Each province has a minimum consumption of 1 ration, even if population is very low.

swap_vert Understanding Net Rations

calculate Net = Production − Consumption

The net rations shown on your country page is:

Net = (Farm Production) − (Population Consumption)

Positive NetYou're stockpiling rations ✓
Zero NetBreaking even
Negative NetRations decreasing ⚠️

lightbulb Tips for Positive Net

  • Build more farms (more production)
  • Acquire more land (higher per-farm output)
  • Increase productivity (universities, libraries)
  • Research Advanced Machinery upgrade
  • Buy rations from the market

help FAQ

sync_problem Why Are My Rations Going Down?

  • Check your net: Is it positive or negative?
  • Build more farms: Match consumption with production
  • Timing: Rations update at :25 (add) and :45 (subtract)
  • Refresh: Wait for both tasks to run before judging

warning Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting the per-province minimum consumption
  • Not accounting for low productivity provinces
  • Checking rations between :45 and :25 (only consumption visible)
  • Not having enough money to operate farms ($3k/farm/hr)
