Can’t reach the site? Try these steps

  • Switch networks: try toggling Wi‑Fi and mobile data.
  • Use a different DNS resolver: set DNS to 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare) or 8.8.8.8 (Google).
  • Restart the browser or device to clear DNS cache.
  • Disable VPNs or corporate proxies that may block resolution.
  • If the problem persists, open this page and paste the error text in a support ticket so we can investigate (include time and your ISP/mobile operator).
