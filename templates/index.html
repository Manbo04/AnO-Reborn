Establish Your Country

Build your nation from the ground up. Collect taxes from your citizens, declare war on enemies, and establish an economy by buying and selling resources on a global market. Compete with real people to become the best.

Join a Coalition

Work with friends to build dominant factions. Engage in politics with allies; trade and establish treaties to rise to the top. Raid countries together, share resources, and fight in vigorous battles against other coalitions.

Customize Your Economy

Resource Production

Produce resources such as food, oil, coal, uranium, and wood to sell on the market to other players.

Eventually build up a strong economy to support production of advanced resources, such as components, steel, ammunition, aluminium, and gasoline.

Tax Collection

Try to keep your people happy, healthy and fed. Build stores and public works to accomplish this, and attempt to maximize your tax income.

Once you can produce enough money, purchase consumer goods and provide them to your population to make more money.

War Looting

Brandish your firearms and wage war against other players. Build up tanks, bombers, destroyers, submarines and much more to crush your enemies and steal their resources.

When you have enough resources, build up missiles and nukes and send them aloft to watch your adversaries burn.
