warning CRITICAL: Distribution System

You must have Distribution Centers to use Rations and Consumer Goods.

Even if you produce 1 million kg of rations, NOT A SINGLE CITIZEN WILL CONSUME THEM without distribution infrastructure.

Distribution Ratio1 building per 50,000 citizens
Rations BuildingsGas Stations, General Stores, Farmers Markets, Malls
Consumer Goods BuildingsMalls, General Stores, Gas Stations
Effect of Missing DistributionPopulation doesn't grow, happiness drops, taxes plummet
Rule of ThumbBuild distribution infrastructure FIRST, then production
