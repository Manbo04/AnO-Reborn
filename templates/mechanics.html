Consumer goods are advanced manufactured items that increase tax income. Keep supply roughly 1 per 80k people to maximize tax yield.

Resources & Uses

Resources (e.g., rations, oil, coal, iron, lumber, components, etc.) are produced by provinces and consumed for building infrastructure, military, and projects.
