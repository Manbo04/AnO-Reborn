Welcome to AnO - Your Journey Begins!

Follow these steps to learn the basics of nation management, economy building, and warfare in AnO.

#1 Create Your First Province

Provinces are the foundation of your nation. They provide population, resources, and infrastructure. Start by creating your first province to establish your economic base.

#2 Understand Your Resources

Check your country page to see your resources: gold, manpower, food, coal, oil, uranium, and more. These resources are generated by your provinces and used for military units, upgrades, and trading.

north_east View Resources

#3 Build Your Military

Military units are essential for defense and conquest. Use your manpower and resources to recruit infantry, tanks, aircraft, and naval vessels. Your military strength determines your position in the world.

north_east Military Page

#4 Explore the Market

The market is where you can buy and sell resources. Check current prices, place offers, and manage your trades. Strategic trading can help you acquire rare resources or generate gold income.

north_east Visit Market

#5 Research Upgrades

Upgrades improve your nation's capabilities. Research better military technology, economic boosts, and infrastructure improvements. Each upgrade requires resources and time to complete.

north_east View Upgrades

#6 Find War Targets

War is a core part of AnO. Find suitable targets based on your military strength. Consider their defenses, alliances, and resources before declaring war. Victory brings territory and spoils.

north_east Find Targets

#7 Join a Coalition

Coalitions provide protection, shared resources, and diplomatic advantages. Join an existing coalition or create your own. Alliances can be crucial for survival in competitive wars.

#8 Advanced Strategies

Master the art of diplomacy, economic planning, and military tactics. Monitor your nation's statistics, manage policies, and adapt to changing circumstances. The most successful nations balance growth with strategic warfare.
