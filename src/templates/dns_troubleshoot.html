Can’t reach the site? Try these steps

  • Switch networks: Toggle between Wi‑Fi and mobile data, or try a different Wi‑Fi network.
  • Change DNS settings:
    • iOS: Settings > Wi-Fi > Tap your network > Configure DNS > Manual > Add 1.1.1.1 or 8.8.8.8.
    • Android: Settings > Network & internet > Advanced > Private DNS > Private DNS provider hostname > Enter dns.adguard.com or p2.freedns.controld.com.
    • Windows: Settings > Network & Internet > Change adapter options > Right-click your connection > Properties > Internet Protocol Version 4 > Properties > Use the following DNS server addresses > Preferred: 8.8.8.8, Alternate: 8.8.4.4.
  • Clear cache and restart: Close the browser, clear browser cache, or restart your device.
  • Disable VPN/proxy: Turn off any VPN or corporate proxy that may interfere.
  • Check DNS externally: Visit dnschecker.org and enter "affairsandorder.com" to see if it resolves globally.
  • If the problem persists, note the exact error, time, and your location/ISP, then contact support.
